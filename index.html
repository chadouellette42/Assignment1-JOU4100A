The Heart of Ottawa

Ottawa is wonderful city and also happens to be the nation's capital. From a distance, it may seem like a government city that isn't very "fun." When people speak of Canada, it's always of Montreal, or Toronto, or even Calgary. I took a tour around Ottawa last year to document Ottawa's diverse nature, and attempted to prove that Ottawa is the place to be.
